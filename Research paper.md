## Abstract

Hospitals often face operational inefficiencies such as poor bed occupancy management, uneven staff allocation, underutilized equipment, delayed patient movement, and lack of emergency preparedness. Existing systems mainly focus on monitoring rather than intelligent optimization. To address this issue, we developed **MedFlow Nexus**, an AI-powered healthcare intelligence platform that predicts demand, automates workflows, and maximizes available resources.

The system supports two modes:

- **Regional Intelligence Mode** for district/statewide healthcare planning  
- **My Hospital Mode** for hospital-specific optimization using internal data  

MedFlow Nexus integrates forecasting models, resource optimization, emergency planning, insurance intelligence, and digital twin simulation into one platform. Testing with realistic hospital datasets showed improved bed utilization, reduced idle assets, lower wait time, and better decision support. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
